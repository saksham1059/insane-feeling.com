IS IT THE END?

Some of the voices in my head screams
A loss which frights me even in dreams
A misunderstanding created a hole
Someone's manupulations too played a great role
  Everyone gained but i was the one who lost
My trust was broken by the one i loved most
Loosing my best friend made me loose my sanity
As he only tried to defend his own vanity
  I thought him to be my friend forever
But forever turned too small however
My best friend was supposed to handle me everytime
So how could i handle the fact that my BEST FRIEND was not mine ?
  Yeah i annoyed you and you used to piss me off
We used to fight and we used to scoff
I used to get mad and pretend that i wouldn't talk
And you used to pamper me until i held your hand to walk
  Where did all that love and care go ?
For the first time i asked you to wait and you said "NO"
One misunderstanding and we were no longer BEST FRIENDS
Is this how our bond and our friendship ends ?
IS THIS HOW IT ALL ENDS ??
